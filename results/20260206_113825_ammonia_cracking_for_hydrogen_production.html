  • Scalability and Momentum: Industrial-scale demonstrations are underway (e.g., KBR in South Korea, Air Liquide in Belgium), proving the technology at a meaningful scale (10-30 tons H‚ÇÇ/day) and derisking key components.

Final Verdict & Next Steps

Verdict: Strategic Pilot, Not Commercial Roll-Out.

The technology is not yet commercially viable for bulk hydrogen supply. However, its strategic value in enabling a global hydrogen trade is too significant to abandon.
